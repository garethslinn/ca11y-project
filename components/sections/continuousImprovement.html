6. Continuous Improvement

6.1 Collecting Feedback

  • 6.1.1 Candidate Surveys

    • Send anonymous post-application surveys to gather feedback on the accessibility and inclusivity of the recruitment process.

  • 6.1.2 Interview Follow-Ups

    • Ask candidates directly about their experience and whether they encountered any barriers during the process.

  • 6.1.3 Diversity Metrics

    • Analyse recruitment data to identify patterns and areas for improvement, such as candidate demographics, success rates, or feedback trends.

  • 6.1.4 Iterative Adjustments

    • Use collected feedback to make ongoing adjustments, ensuring the process evolves to meet diverse needs.

6.2 Regular Review of Practices

  • 6.2.1 Annual Audits

    • Conduct regular reviews of recruitment practices to identify gaps and align with the latest accessibility standards and best practices.

  • 6.2.2 Policy Updates

    • Revise recruitment policies and documentation to reflect changes in legal requirements, technology, or industry standards.

  • 6.2.3 Stakeholder Engagement

    • Collaborate with employees, candidates, and advocacy groups to gather insights and refine inclusivity strategies.

  • 6.2.4 Training Refreshers

    • Schedule periodic training sessions for HR and hiring managers to ensure awareness of updated practices and techniques.
