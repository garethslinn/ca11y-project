A. Sample Inclusive Job Descriptions

Below are examples of job descriptions that incorporate inclusive language and accessibility features:

  • Software Developer: "We welcome applicants from diverse backgrounds and encourage those with disabilities to apply. If you require accommodations during the hiring process, please let us know."
  • Customer Service Representative: "This role involves assisting customers via phone and email. Training materials are provided in accessible formats, and flexible work arrangements are available."

B. Interview Checklist for Accessibility

Use this checklist to ensure your interview process is inclusive and accessible:

  1. Confirm interview location accessibility (e.g., ramps, elevators, quiet spaces).
  2. Provide clear directions to the interview site or virtual meeting link.
  3. Ensure assistive technologies are available if requested.
  4. Share the interview agenda and sample questions in advance.
  5. Offer flexible formats, such as virtual interviews or alternative timing.
  6. Ensure interviewers are trained in inclusivity and bias awareness.
