7. Resources and Training

7.1 Additional Resources

  • 7.1.1 Web Accessibility Standards

    • Provide links to resources like WCAG guidelines, accessibility testing tools, and assistive technology guides.

  • 7.1.2 Inclusive Hiring Frameworks

    • Share frameworks and toolkits from organisations focused on diversity and inclusion in recruitment.

  • 7.1.3 Community Support Groups

    • List local or online communities that support candidates with disabilities or neurodivergence.

  • 7.1.4 Government Resources

    • Include links to government publications and support programs related to disability and employment.

7.2 Training Opportunities

  • 7.2.1 Workshops and Seminars

    • Offer internal or external workshops on inclusive hiring practices, focusing on accessibility, unconscious bias, and neurodiversity.

  • 7.2.2 Accessibility Certification Programs

    • Encourage HR professionals to pursue certifications in accessibility and inclusive design.

  • 7.2.3 Scenario-Based Training

    • Provide role-playing exercises or case studies to simulate real-world scenarios and improve decision-making in inclusive recruitment.

  • 7.2.4 Ongoing Learning Platforms

    • Recommend online courses or e-learning platforms to build a culture of continuous education around accessibility and inclusion.
